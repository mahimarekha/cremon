The (Montessori) teacher's task is not a small (or) easy one! She has to prepare a huge amount of knowledge to satisfy the child's mental hunger. She is not like the ordinary teacher, limited by syllabus. The needs of the child are clearly more difficult to answer.
​
- MARIA MONTESSORI
​Vinaya Palagiri Founder, Primary & Elementary Guide
Vinaya is the visionary behind Cremon, and the catalyst in bringing us all into the family. A typical story of a parent trying to find a school which values the freedom of child, combined with the passion to childhood education has transformed into Cremon over ten years ago against all odds. Her thirst to learn about childhood education has led her to complete diploma in Pre-Primary Education from IMC, Pre-Primary and Elementary level courses from NAMC (North American Montessori Centre), a diploma in Elementary Education from AMI’s Montessori Institute Prague, and training on Infancy development in Glenn Doman method. She has her sights set on establishing the Montessori way of Adolescent Education – ‘Erd Kinder’ to provide a more complete environment for children.
Bilquis Jahan Hindi Guide
Bilquis is a mother figure to us all at Cremon. She gives us wisdom and watches over us all, in addition to teaching Hindi. She has worked with traditional schools for over 25 years and then transitioned to Montessori schools about 6 years ago. She holds degrees for B.Ed and MA in Hindi. She has experienced how a child’s own understanding of concepts leads to holistic development compared to rote memorization, this practice has led her to appreciate and stay in Montessori. She is passionate about working with children and traveling.
Kavita Chada Permaculture & Farming Guide
Miss Kavita obtained a B.Sc. in Mathematics from Pune University.  She had an inclination towards sports and has represented her college in inter University football tournaments. Since 2001 she has been living in Hyderabad. She worked for a Private company as Sales & Service coordinator for 18years. In 2018, she has had enough and resigned from her job as she did not get the job satisfaction. She had interest to learn about chemical free farming and an alternative way of living, and went on a search for some courses in these fields.  She learnt about Permaculture and did a Permaculture Design Course in 2018 from Aranya Permaculture Academy, Zaheerabad. She is currently working, learning and exploring at Janani Farms near Hyderabad. Cremon supports sustainability and principles of permaculture and has thus acquainted with Miss Kavita to get our elementary children hands into the soil for farming. She says working with children is a little bit of sharing knowledge going both ways. She talks about the importance of soil, seeds, plants and trees in everyone's life. She feels "we learn more when we explore nature because it's the best teacher".

Nabanita Porel Elementary Assistant Guide

Rama Chandra Reddy Permaculturist & Sustainability Guide
Mr. Rama Chandra hails from an agriculture family. His is a story of passion and realization. His education in engineering is a far cry from the life he leads on a farm today. He worked for 10 years in public sector, 5 years in the State Government and 10 years as a contractor. During this time he saw first hand how unethical practices such as bribery lead to "pollution of society". He then decided that making a living ethically is more important than profits derived otherwise. In his research he found that Permaculture (hybrid of two words - Permanent Agriculture) is a practice with ethics/principles at its core. He was immediately inspired by them, as they help him craft his own life ethically, to produce food ethically and protect natural resources in the process - a winning combination. His commitment to these principles has won him recognition from Medchal-Malkajgiri District as Best Farmer. He did his permaculture course at Aranya Permaculture Farm in Zaheerabad and Permaculture Teacher Training course with Jude Hobbs. He now practices ethical farming using these principles at his farm near Uppal and teaches and guides students at schools and anyone else interested.

Venkata Suresh Jannu Telugu Guide
