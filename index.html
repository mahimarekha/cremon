reTHINK EDUCATION

We believe that each child is born with the wisdom of a thousand generations past. Our mission is to nurture their innate abilities and talents. At Cremon, we strive to keep 'that spark' shining bright in every child.
• OUR VISION •

When the child is free, learning comes easy.

There is a reason that the philosophy of an Italian educator - Dr. Maria Montessori, has endured the test of time for over a century, and is thriving around the world. It is the belief that nature is in the child as much as the child is in the nature and that learning in a child doesn't happen just in school, it happens everywhere, all the time, and it cannot be separated from the child.
The function of a true school, we believe is thus, not just to impart lessons, but to embark on a journey with the child. A journey of curiosity, of discovery and amazement, and of learning.
A true educator observes the child, tries to understand their nature and needs to provide the right support at the right time.

The Songs of Life

Everything children encounter, we believe, adds to their bank of memories.
At Cremon we call these "The Songs of Life".

Songs that have a definite beginning but no end.
We all keep composing these songs instinctively, guided by the hand of creation;
sometimes wide awake, sometimes dreaming but always alert;
sometimes happy, sometimes sad but always feeling.

Every person we meet adds to the tune.
Some teach us humility, some grace,
some composure and some peace.

We realize that life is not a rat race to be won
but a journey to be experienced.
